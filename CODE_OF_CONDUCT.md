# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We expect contributors and maintainers to treat one another with respect and professionalism.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and factual.
- Respect the repository's licensing and contribution rules.
- Avoid harassment, abuse, or disruptive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination.
- Deliberate license violations.
- Unauthorized distribution of restricted assets.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict access, or take other corrective action when necessary to protect the repository and its contributors.
